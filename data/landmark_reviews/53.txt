"A dependable biryani-and-mandi name in Feb 2026. Halais has spread to a few branches across the city, but the formula is the same everywhere — solid dum biryani and authentic-style Arabian grills at fair prices. Feb 2026"

"SIGNATURE DISHES: Kuzhi mandi is the trademark — chicken or mutton over smoky rice, slow-cooked. The dum biryani and the al-faham (charcoal grilled chicken) are the other regulars. Grilled items are consistently well prepared. Jan 2026"

"PRICING & VALUE: Around Rs 350 for two — strong value, which is why it's a student and office favourite. 2025"

"AMBIANCE & CROWD: Casual, fast, no-frills, with a heavy home-delivery business. Rated about 3.7. It's a workhorse eatery rather than a destination — come for the food, not the setting. Oct 2025"

"LOGISTICS: Original at Vanchiyoor/Palayam, with branches near Technopark/Kazhakootam and Infosys. Open roughly 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Free home delivery is available across much of the city. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: If you can't decide between biryani and mandi, the kuzhi mandi is the move — it's what they're known for. Good option to order in if you'd rather eat at your hotel after a long day. 2024"
