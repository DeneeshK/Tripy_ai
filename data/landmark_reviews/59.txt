NAME: Aruvikkara Dam
CATEGORY: Dam & River Spot / Picnic & Temple

[RAW_REVIEW_REPOSITORY]

"A relaxed family picnic spot on the Karamana river in Feb 2026. Aruvikkara is where Trivandrum families go to unwind by the water after a busy week — a small dam, a pretty riverside park, and an old temple all in one place. Feb 2026"

"THE TEMPLE & FISH: Beside the dam is an ancient rock-cut temple to Goddess Durga, believed to date to around the 8th century. The river here is full of fish, and feeding them is a long-standing ritual — kids love it. Jan 2026"

"WHAT TO DO: Picnicking is the main activity, with a small but pleasant park for families and young children. On most weekends the park and dam fill up with families. Light boating is sometimes available. Oct 2025"

"AMBIANCE & CROWD: Quiet and green on weekdays, livelier on weekends. Reviewers call it a perfect place to chill out with family. Afternoon into evening is the recommended window. 2025"

"LOGISTICS: About 16 km north of Trivandrum on the banks of the Karamana river. Open roughly daylight hours (around 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM). Ample parking, buses available, and a food counter operating at select times. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: Carry your own snacks/picnic since on-site food is limited, and feed the fish near the temple steps — it's the local thing to do. Pairs well with Koyikkal Palace, which is on the same northern side. 2024"
