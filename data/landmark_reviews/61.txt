NAME: Thiruvallam Parasurama Temple
CATEGORY: Riverside Temple / Backwaters & Canoeing

[RAW_REVIEW_REPOSITORY]

"A tranquil temple-and-backwater stop in Feb 2026. Thiruvallam sits at the Triveni Sangamam — the meeting of the Karamana and Killi rivers and the Parvathy Puthanar canal — and is the only temple in Kerala dedicated to Lord Parasurama. Feb 2026"

"THE TEMPLE: Built around the 12th-13th century in the late-Pandyan period, it's especially associated with ancestral rites (bali tharpanam). Mornings during Karkidakam see large numbers performing these rituals; otherwise it's calm and contemplative. Jan 2026"

"DARSHAN TIMINGS: Two sessions — roughly 4:00 AM to 11:00 AM in the morning and 5:00 PM to 8:20 PM in the evening, with timings extending on festivals and auspicious days. Dress modestly as at any Kerala temple. Oct 2025"

"BACKWATERS & CANOEING: The real surprise is the setting — quiet backwaters you can explore by canoe or kayak, with short traditional boat rides on offer. It's one of the closest backwater experiences to the city. 2025"

"LOGISTICS: Thiruvallam, about 6-8 km south of Trivandrum centre on the Karamana river. Easily paired with Kovalam or Vellayani. The canoe/boat operators are near the temple ghat. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: Come for the early-evening session, then take a short canoe ride as the light softens over the confluence. Combine with a Kovalam sunset for a full southern-side afternoon. 2024"
