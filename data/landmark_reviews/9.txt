ID: 9
NAME: Shangumugham Beach
CATEGORY: City Beach / Public Park

[RAW_REVIEW_REPOSITORY]

"The 'Airport Beach.' It is literally adjacent to the Trivandrum International Airport. A perfect spot to spend 1-2 hours before catching a flight. March 2023"

"Iconic Landmark: Don't miss the 'Sagarakanyaka' (Giant Mermaid Sculpture) by Kanayi Kunjiraman. It’s the most photographed spot on the beach. There is also a star-shaped restaurant and an indoor recreation club nearby. 2024"

"Local Vibe: Unlike Kovalam, this is where the locals hang out. It’s perfect for 'people watching.' You’ll see fishermen pulling in nets in the early morning and hundreds of families watching the sunset in the evening. 2025"

"Safety Warning: The waves here are much stronger and the shore drops off steeply compared to Kovalam. Swimming is generally NOT recommended and often barred by life guards. It’s a beach for walking and sitting, not bathing. 2025"

"Food & Culture: Excellent street food scene in the evenings. Lots of 'reddiwalas' selling spicy snacks. The Indian Coffee House here is a classic spot for a quick bite with a view. 2024"

"Sunset & Ambience: The sunset view over the Arabian Sea is breathtaking. The white sandy stretch is long and provides a great sense of the 'majestic presence' of the sea. Nov 2023"

"Facilities: There is a Children's Traffic Park (Jawaharlal Nehru Park) nearby which is great for kids. Seating arrangements and benches are available under tree shades, though parking can be a nightmare on Sunday evenings. Jan 2024"

"Cleanliness: Generally a neat and clean beach, but being a city beach, it can get crowded and litter can be an issue right after a festival or a busy weekend. 2025"

"Spiritual Note: A renovated Hindu Devi temple is right across the road. During the 'Arattu' festival, the temple deities are brought here for a holy dip in the sea. 2024"
