"PRICING & VALUE: Light on the wallet — roughly Rs 100-200 per person. Easy add-on after dinner. 2025"

"AMBIANCE & CROWD: Casual, family-friendly, busiest in the evenings and after dinner hours. Two-wheeler parking right in front. It's a grab-a-dessert spot, not a sit-for-hours cafe. Oct 2025"

"LOGISTICS: Sasthamangalam, very near Indira Bhavan, Trivandrum. Open roughly 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Also available on Swiggy and other delivery apps. 2024"
