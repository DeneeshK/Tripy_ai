NAME: Puliyarakonam Hills (Terumo Penpol Road)
CATEGORY: Scenic Drive / Viewpoint / Local Hangout

[RAW_REVIEW_REPOSITORY]

"A favorite evening escape for locals in Feb 2026. The road leading past the Terumo Penpol factory up towards Puliyarakonam offers some of the most underrated sunset views of the city's outskirts. It’s quiet, green, and perfect for a slow drive. Feb 2026"

"THE VIBE: This isn't a tourist 'spot' with tickets or gates. It’s a stretch of road with elevated curves where you can pull over and see the valley below. It's very popular with motorcyclists and young people from the city looking for a 'hill station' feel without driving all the way to Ponmudi. Jan 2026"

"LOCATION: About 12km from the city center. The landmark everyone uses is the Terumo Penpol factory. The air quality here is noticeably better than the city, and the temperature usually feels a degree or two cooler in the evenings. 2025"

"LOGISTICS: No entry fee. No fixed timings, though it’s best visited between 5:00 PM and 6:30 PM. Parking is just on the side of the road, so be careful of passing traffic. There are a few small local 'thattukada' (street food stalls) that pop up in the evening selling tea and snacks. Oct 2025"

"CAUTION: The roads are narrow and have several sharp bends. During the monsoon, the mist can get quite thick, which is beautiful but makes driving a bit hazardous. There is no street lighting in some stretches, so driving back after dark requires focus. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: If you continue past the factory further into the hills, you'll find small hidden trails that lead to higher points. It's a great spot for amateur landscape photography. 2024"
