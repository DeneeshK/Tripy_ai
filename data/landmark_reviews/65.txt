"The grand royal landmark of the city in Feb 2026. Kowdiar Palace, built in 1934, is the residence of the Travancore royal family — a sprawling building set on a green hillock in the upmarket Kowdiar neighbourhood. Feb 2026"

"IMPORTANT — ACCESS: This is a PRIVATE RESIDENCE and is NOT open to the public. You cannot go inside. Visitors view and photograph the grand exterior from the main gate only — treat it as a short photo-and-architecture stop, not a tour. Jan 2026"

"THE BUILDING: Originally gifted by Maharaja Chithira Thirunal Balarama Varma to his sister Princess Karthika Thirunal on her wedding, the palace blends traditional Kerala architecture with European elements. Even from the gate the scale and the manicured approach are impressive. Oct 2025"

"AMBIANCE: The Kowdiar area around it is leafy and affluent, pleasant for a short walk. Gate-viewing hours are commonly cited as roughly 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM, but since it's a residence, access can be limited at any time. 2025"

"LOGISTICS: Kowdiar Gardens, about 4 km from the city centre — very central and easy to reach. A few minutes is enough unless you're combining it with a stroll around Kowdiar. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: Keep expectations right — this is a 'see the famous gate, take a photo, move on' landmark, best slotted between nearby stops like Kanakakunnu Palace or a Vellayambalam meal rather than visited on its own. 2024"
