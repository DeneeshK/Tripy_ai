"A rewarding little waterfall trek in Feb 2026. Meenmutty, on the Kallar river just below Ponmudi, is a favourite day-out for families and friends — a mild forest trek that ends at cool natural pools. Rated about 4.4 across 3600+ reviews. Feb 2026"

"THE TREK: The walk starts at Kallar village and runs around 2-3 km downstream through forest, taking roughly an hour at an easy-to-moderate pace. The path follows the river, so it's scenic the whole way. Jan 2026"

"THE FALLS & POOLS: The falls themselves are modest, but the draw is the cluster of small natural rock pools with smooth round boulders and colourful pebbles — chilled water that's perfect for a long dip. Many visitors spend a couple of hours just bathing. Oct 2025"

"TIMINGS & ENTRY: Open roughly 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM; best season November to March. Small entry fee (around Rs 30 per adult, Rs 10 for children, Rs 30 for a vehicle). 2025"

"LOGISTICS: Kallar, about 45 km from Trivandrum (and ~16 km from Ponmudi bus station) on the Vithura-Ponmudi road. Footwear with grip helps on the wet rocks. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: Go in the morning, wear sandals you can walk and wade in, and don't venture onto the slippery upper rocks near the main fall — the lower pools are where the fun (and safety) is. Easy to club with Ponmudi or Peppara. 2024"
