"LOGISTICS: Second-floor dining area, Lulu Mall, Akkulam side, Thiruvananthapuram — about 8 km from Trivandrum Central. Open roughly 11:00 AM to 10:45 PM, in step with mall hours. Free mall parking. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: Best paired with a Lulu Mall trip rather than a special detour. Go heavy on the kebabs and burra — that's where the Old-Delhi pedigree really shows. 2024"
