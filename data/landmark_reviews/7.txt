"CRITICAL SAFETY WARNING: DO NOT USE THE ELEVATOR. In Oct 2025, a family was trapped halfway for an hour with sparks flying and a lack of air. Faulty system with multiple reported failures. Management described as poor. Oct 2025"

"Breathtaking 360-degree views of Vizhinjam Harbour, Kovalam beach, and the city. If visiting during monsoon, be prepared for very strong winds. Open from 2:00 PM (after lunch break). Sept 2025"

"Guide for Visitors: Parking is available on-site (ticket required). Entry tickets required for individuals; phone cameras are free, but professional cameras are charged. Two ways up: 140 spiral steps or a small elevator (fits 2-3 people). The elevator only goes 'almost' to the top; you must still climb steep steps and a metal ladder (monkey ladder) to reach the deck. Staff controls flow on the ladder. Small cafe and sea-view playground on premises. July 2025"

"Great for senior citizens—even though the elevator doesn't go to the very top, the viewing deck is only a few steep steps away from the lift exit. Offers views of the crescent-shaped beaches. Jan 2025"

"Worth the wait even if the queue is long. If climbing, windows provide a breeze. Drinking water station available at the entrance. Stuffy inside the stairwell. Closed on Mondays. Nov 2024"

"Logistics: Entry is very cheap (approx Rs 10-20). Open 10:00 AM – 12:30 PM and 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM. Note the midday closure. Must remove shoes before climbing. Underdevelopment: Musical fountain and expanded play area. April 2024"

"The nearby Vizhinjam area is famous for seafood dishes, though some local hotel restaurants have received poor reviews for service and food quality. Sept 2023"
