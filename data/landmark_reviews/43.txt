"One of the oldest running eateries in the city, still going strong in Feb 2026. People queue up to get a seat — that tells you everything. The Palayam branch on PMG Road is the original draw. Feb 2026"

"SIGNATURE DISHES: The 'Kethel's' chicken fry is the legend here — made with fresh chicken, minimal spices and pure coconut oil, so the flavour is clean and the chicken itself does the work. Have it with their porotta or a plate of ghee rice. The chicken biryani is a solid backup. Jan 2026"

"PRICING & VALUE: Around Rs 400-450 for two. Old-school pricing for an old-school place. 2025"

"AMBIANCE & CROWD: No-frills, functional, often crowded — you come for the chicken fry, not the decor. Rated about 3.8 over 4700+ reviews. Expect a short wait at peak dinner. Oct 2025"

"LOGISTICS: PMG Road, Palayam. Open roughly 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Central, but parking around Palayam is tight; an auto is easiest. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: Order the chicken fry the moment you sit — it's made fresh and the popular batches go fast in the evening. Locals say the magic is the coconut oil, so don't ask them to tone it down. 2024"
