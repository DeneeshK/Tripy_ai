"Still Trivandrum's most dependable veg meal in Feb 2026. Right next to Thampanoor railway station at Aristo Junction, so it's the first proper meal most travellers grab when they step off a train. Clean, quick, and unfussy. Feb 2026"

"SIGNATURE DISHES: The South Indian breakfast is the reason to come — ghee roast, masala dosa, idli-vada and a genuinely good filter coffee. Their unlimited vegetarian thali (meals) at lunch is excellent value. They also do North Indian and a small Chinese section, but stick to the South Indian side. Jan 2026"

"PRICING & VALUE: Very budget-friendly — a full meal for two comes to around Rs 250-400. This is everyday-eating pricing, not a special-occasion spot. 2025"

"AMBIANCE & CROWD: Functional and busy. There's an AC hall and a non-AC hall. Because it's beside the station it gets a constant churn of travellers; turnover is fast so you rarely wait long even when it looks full. Rated 4.1 over 2600+ reviews. Oct 2025"

"LOGISTICS: Manorama Road, Aristo Junction, near Thampanoor. Open roughly 6:30 AM to 10:30 PM daily — one of the few places already serving a hot breakfast that early. Easy to reach on foot from the station or KSRTC bus stand. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: Come for an early breakfast before a day of sightseeing — it opens at 6:30 AM when almost nothing else is ready. Ask for the ghee podi dosa if it's on; locals rate it. 2024"
