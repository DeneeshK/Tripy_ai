"A true escape from the city. I visited in Feb 2026 and was one of only five people on the entire stretch. The coconut groves give it a very tropical, untouched feel. If you're tired of the crowds at Kovalam, this is where you go. Feb 2026"

"LOCAL LIFE: Watching the fishermen bring in their nets is a highlight. You can even buy fresh fish directly on the shore if you're there at the right time. It's a very authentic glimpse into the coastal life of Kerala. Jan 2026"

"AMENITIES WARNING: Don't expect cafes or loungers. There's a small shop near the entrance, but that's about it. Bring your own mat and snacks. The lack of commercialization is exactly what makes it special. Oct 2025"

"HISTORICAL NOTE: The sand mounds/hills are fascinating once you know their history. They supposedly date back to the colonial era for cannon placements. It adds a bit of mystery to a simple beach day. 2025"

"SAFETY TIP: The water is beautiful but can be deceptive. Without lifeguards, it's best to stay in the shallow areas. It's perfect for a quiet morning of meditation or yoga. 2024"
