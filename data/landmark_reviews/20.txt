"Spectacular views from the top of the dam in Feb 2026. The water gushing down with the gates open is a photographer's dream. It’s a peaceful escape from the city, and the fresh breeze near the reservoir is amazing. Feb 2026"

"BOATING & SAFARI: The boat ride is mandatory to reach the Lion Safari Park and the Crocodile Rehabilitation Center. Safari Boat: Rs 400 (20 min); Speed Boat: Rs 250. Note: Spotting a lion is rare as there are only a few left in the semi-wild enclosure, but the boat ride itself is calming and scenic. Jan 2025"

"CROCODILE PARK: The Crocodile Rehabilitation and Research Centre is home to over 40 crocodiles. You can see them lazing in naturalistic settings. It’s one of the best-maintained parts of the dam complex. Entry: Rs 30 (Adult). Oct 2024"

"KOTTOOR ELEPHANT CENTER: Located about 10km from the dam (usually bundled in the same trip). Arrive by 9:00 AM to watch the elephants being bathed in the river and 10:30 AM for feeding. This is the highlight for most visitors. Entry: Rs 50. Feb 2025"

"FACILITIES: There is a watchtower for panoramic views, a small aquarium, and a well-maintained children’s park. However, some signage is missing, and the cafeteria options are quite basic. Carry your own snacks. 2024"

"TIMINGS & LOGISTICS: Closed on MONDAYS. Open 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM (Dam) / 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM (Safari). Parking is ample but carries a small fee. It’s best to hire a taxi for the day from Trivandrum as public transport to the interior dam area is infrequent. 2025"

"WARNING: The reservoir is known to have crocodiles; do not attempt to swim in any unrestricted areas. Stick to the designated boating zones. 2024"
