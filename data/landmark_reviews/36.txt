NAME: Karikkakom Sree Chamundi Temple
CATEGORY: Hindu Temple / Heritage Site

[RAW_REVIEW_REPOSITORY]

"A powerful spiritual center in Feb 2026. The unique thing about this temple is the three different sanctums for the three forms of the Goddess. The atmosphere is very traditional and the rituals are performed with great discipline. Feb 2026"

"THE TRUTH OATH: Historically, the Raktha Chamundi shrine was used as a 'court' where people would take an oath to prove their innocence in disputes. Even today, people visit with deep faith that the Goddess will bring justice to their lives. Jan 2026"

"VISITOR INFO: 
- Timings: 5:00 AM – 10:00 AM and 5:30 PM – 8:30 PM (Sundays have extended morning hours until 12:00 PM).
- Dress Code: Like most Kerala temples, traditional attire is required. Men must remove their shirts to enter the inner sanctum. 2025"

"FESTIVAL VIBE: The Karikkakom Pongala is the second largest in the city after Attukal. The entire area turns into a massive hearth with women cooking offerings in clay pots. It’s an incredible sight but very crowded. 2025"

"LOCATION & ACCESS: Located right near the NH Bypass. It’s very easy to reach by car or auto. There is a decent amount of parking space available compared to the temples in the city center. 2024"

"LOCAL TIP: The temple has a very peaceful pond area. It’s a great place to sit for a few minutes after the darshan to soak in the quiet energy of the place. 2024"
